Welcome To JeffShredsFoamies

Sufing With JDizzle

At JeffShredsFoamies, we believe surfing should be fun, approachable, and something anyone can enjoy—no pressure, just good vibes. Led by Jeff, foam board enthusiast and wave-chasing coach, we specialize in helping beginners find their balance and experience the joy of catching their first wave. Whether you are standing tall or laughing through a splash, JeffShredsFoamies is here to make sure every ride is a good time.

Surf Lessons

Whether it is your very first time or you are brushing up on your skills, JeffShredsFoamies will help you go from wobbly knees to riding waves with confidence.

Board Basics

From foamies to hardtops, we will walk you through everything you need to know—paddling, popping up, and keeping your board waxed and ready.

Ocean Safety

Learn how to read the waves, understand riptides, and practice safe surfing habits so every session is fun and safe.
